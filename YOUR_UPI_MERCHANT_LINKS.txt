HOW TO GET YOUR 'aid' (App ID):

Option 1: ICICI Bank (Your Bank)
─────────────────────────────────
1. Call ICICI Bank customer care: 1860-120-7777
2. Request "UPI Merchant Registration" or "UPI Merchant Services"
3. They will guide you through the process
4. You'll receive your App ID (aid) after registration

Option 2: Payment Gateway (Easier)
───────────────────────────────────
1. Register with Razorpay (razorpay.com)
   - Sign up as merchant
   - Complete KYC
   - They provide UPI merchant credentials including 'aid'

2. Or use Paytm for Business
   - Register at paytm.com/business
   - Get merchant UPI credentials
   - Includes App ID

3. Or PhonePe for Business
   - Register at phonepe.com/business
   - Merchant onboarding provides App ID

Option 3: NPCI Direct
──────────────────────
1. Visit npci.org.in
2. Register as UPI merchant
3. Get official App ID from NPCI
